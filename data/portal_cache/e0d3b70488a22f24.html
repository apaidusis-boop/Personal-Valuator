 Environmental, Social, Governance

“Our objective is balanced top- and bottom-line growth. While that will always be an imperative, the world needs more from P&G now. It is not just top and bottom-line growth that must be delivered and balanced. We must also endeavor to deliver against the needs of an increasing number of constituents – consumers, retail customers, employees, society and our shareowners. The needs of each of these constituents must be met. This is particularly important when it comes to environmental, social and governance areas.”  Jon R. Moeller, P&G Chairman of the Board, President & CEO
