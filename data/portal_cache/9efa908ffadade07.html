About Nucor

Nucor is North America’s largest and most diversified steel and steel products producer. As the largest recycler of any material in North America, we’re also one of the most sustainable manufacturers in North America. For over 50 years Nucor has been growth-focused, expanding into new markets to create value for shareholders, teammates, customers & communities. We’ve done this while staying true to our core competency as an efficient manufacturer with a highly variable cost structure, allowing Nucor to generate positive free cash flow consistently through economic cycles.

SUSTAINABILITY

Leading the Way to Net Zero

Nucor has pioneered a circular steelmaking process, transforming recycled scrap into high-quality, low-embodied carbon steel. But we haven't stopped there. We’re one of the first diversified steel producers in North America with science-based, net-zero greenhouse gas targets for 2050, covering Scopes 1, 2, and 3. To achieve this, we’re investing in breakthrough technologies like near zero GHG iron, carbon capture, and a wide range of clean energy solutions.
