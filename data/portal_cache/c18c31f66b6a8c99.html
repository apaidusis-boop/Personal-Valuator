An Uncommon Global Branded Food Company


We are a global branded food company with approximately $12 billion in annual revenue across more than 80 countries worldwide. We have a balanced portfolio including Planters®, Jennie-O®, SPAM®, Applegate®, Skippy®, Wholly®, Hormel®, Black Label® and more than 30 other beloved brands. We have a renowned direct foodservice-selling organization that delivers value to operators and foodservice partners. The company is a member of the S&P 500 Index and the S&P 500 Dividend Aristocrats.
