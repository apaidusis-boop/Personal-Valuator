## Corporate Overview

Church & Dwight
Co., Inc., founded in 1846, is the leading U.S. producer of sodium bicarbonate, popularly known as baking
soda, a natural product that cleans, deodorizes, leavens and buffers. The Company’s ARM & HAMMER brand is
one of the nation’s most trusted trademarks for a broad range of consumer and specialty products developed
from the base of sodium bicarbonate and related technologies.
