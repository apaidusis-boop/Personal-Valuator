# 🎯 Thesis: [[XPML11]] — XP Malls

> FII shopping BR — foot traffic + retail rent. Cíclico ao consumo; DRIP mas não "safe" como logística.

## Intent
**DRIP cyclical** — income + consumer beta BR.

## Business snapshot
- FII tijolo shopping centers mid/upper-tier BR.
- Mix shoppings regionais + premium SP/SE.
- Gestão XP Investimentos.
- DY 8-10% target em mid-cycle.

## Por que detemos

1. **Consumer BR recovery** — Selic descendo + wage growth = foot traffic ↑.
2. **Rent growth real** IPCA + passthrough + occupancy pricing power em prime assets.
3. **Diversificação vs logística + papel** — consumer exposure direct.
4. **Gestão XP** + escala (acesso aos melhores activos primary + secondary sales).

## Moat

- **Prime locations** competitive vs strip malls / DTC (cidades BR ainda shopping-centric).
- **Tenant relationships** ancoradas (major brands querem estes activos).
- **Weak moat vs e-commerce secular** (shift lento mas real long-term).

## Current state (2026-04)

- [[Selic]] descendo → revaluation FII + consumer tailwind
- Vacancy < 5% across portfolio
- Sales/m² recovery vs 2023 stress
- Potential trigger: rent renegotiation cycle (tenant vs landlord power balance shifts em Selic baixo)

## Invalidation triggers

- [ ] Vacancy > 10% portfolio-wide (structural tenant flight)
- [ ] Dividend cut > 20% (economic stress signal)
- [ ] Sales/m² decline > 15% YoY (consumer BR recession)
- [ ] XP gestão change
- [ ] Single major shopping asset sale sem replacement

## Sizing

- Posição actual: 147 cotas
- Target 4-6% sleeve BR
- **Cyclical** — accumulate em Selic peak, trim em Selic bottom
- Current: hold, reinvest para [[BTLG11]] (logística mais secular)
