# 🎯 Thesis: [[BLK]] — BlackRock

> #1 global asset manager ($11T+ AUM). Aladdin software platform + iShares ETF dominance. Compounder fee-based.

## Intent
**Compounder** — fee-on-AUM growth secular. DY bonus, not primary.

## Business snapshot

Three engines:
1. **Asset management** — $11T+ AUM across active, passive (iShares), alternatives.
2. **Aladdin** — risk/portfolio management software platform ($1T+ assets modelled by clients, $1B+ fee revenue).
3. **Advisory + Technology services** (FIG platform acquisition, Aperio).

Largest holder of many S&P 500 constituents globally (passive flows).

## Por que detemos

1. **Fee-based revenue** — recurrring, AUM-linked, low capital requirements.
2. **Aladdin moat** — deeply embedded em institutional investor workflows.
3. **Passive (iShares) secular** — ETF flows taking $500B+/y market share from mutual funds.
4. **Buyback aggressive** — reducing share count consistently.
5. **Dividend growth 13y** (not King yet but tracking).

## Moat

- **Scale economies** — lowest expense ratios iShares (compete on cost = hard to beat).
- **Aladdin switching cost** — asset managers using it for 5+ years lock-in.
- **Regulatory reputation** — BlackRock often consulted on financial stability.
- **Stewardship influence** — proxy votes on 95% S&P 500.
- **Weak moat**: fee compression structural em passive (bp here, bp there).

## Current state (2026-04)

- AUM $11T+ all-time highs post-2024 rally.
- Organic growth rebounding after 2022-23 soft.
- Private markets + infra push (Global Infrastructure Partners acquisition 2024).
- Stewardship political heat reduced vs 2022-23 ESG peak.

## Invalidation triggers

- [ ] AUM decline > 10% sem market beta explanation (structural outflows)
- [ ] Fee rate compression > 2bp/y sustained
- [ ] Aladdin client losses major (top-10 client departure)
- [ ] CEO Larry Fink departure sem succession ready
- [ ] Regulatory break-up pressure executado (unlikely)
- [ ] ROE < 12% structural

## Sizing

- Posição actual: 2 shares
- Target 2-4% sleeve US
- **Compounder** — hold, trim only if valuation extreme (P/E > 30×)
