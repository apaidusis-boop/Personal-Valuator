# 🎯 Thesis: [[NU]] — Nu Holdings (Nubank)

> Largest fintech LatAm. 100M+ customers BR + Mexico + Colombia. **Listed US (NYSE)** mas business 100% LatAm. Growth play em BR financial disruption.

## Intent
**Growth** — capital gain primary. DY zero.

## Business snapshot
- Digital bank native BR (fundada 2013).
- Products: cartão crédito, conta digital, Pix, investimentos, empréstimos, Nubank Ultravioleta (premium), Nubank Business.
- **Customers**: 100M+ BR (50%+ população adulta BR), 10M+ México, 2M+ Colombia.
- Founder/CEO David Vélez (Colombian) + Cristina Junqueira.
- Largest early investor: Sequoia; Berkshire also invested.
- **Listed NYSE** (IPO 2021) — BDR BR existe (NUBR33) mas liquidity concentrated NYSE.

## Por que detemos

1. **BR financial disruption secular** — retail banking incumbents (Itau, Bradesco, Santander) losing share.
2. **Unit economics strong** — CAC < $5 vs ~$200+ traditional banks.
3. **Cross-sell expansion** — original card customer → conta → empréstimo → investimento → seguros.
4. **Geographic expansion** — México + Colombia early innings.
5. **Data moat** — 100M+ customer behavior dataset enables superior credit models.
6. **Profitable** since 2023 (unlike many fintechs).

## Moat

- **Brand** em BR — "pink card" cultural icon.
- **Tech stack** — native cloud, vs incumbent legacy systems.
- **Low-cost acquisition** — referral-driven + viral.
- **Network effects** (Pix + payments ecosystem).
- **Weak moat**: banking regulated → incumbents copying tech (Itaú iti, Inter, C6 competing).

## Current state (2026-04)

- BR customer growth maturing (saturation near-term — 50%+ adults already).
- **Revenue per customer growing** — cross-sell + credit expansion.
- México + Colombia = growth engines 2026-28.
- Credit loss ratio elevated but managing (2023-24 bumpy).
- Stock volatile — growth premium + emerging markets risk combo.

## Invalidation triggers

- [ ] Customer growth < 15%/y sustained (saturation + competition)
- [ ] ARPU declining 2 years (pricing pressure)
- [ ] Credit loss ratio > 7% sustained (aggressive lending backfiring)
- [ ] Major regulatory action BR (Bacen limits)
- [ ] David Vélez departure
- [ ] Operating margin < 10% 2 years (unit economics break)
- [ ] Currency crisis BR/México destroying offshore returns

## Sizing

- Posição actual: 13 shares
- Target 2-4% sleeve US
- **Growth — volatile** — hold but size modesto
- **Duplicate BR exposure** already via ITSA4, BBDC4 — NU concentrates fintech disruption specifically.
