# 🎯 Thesis: [[PG]] — Procter & Gamble

> Dividend King 68y — **longest streak S&P 500**. Best-in-class working capital management. Core DRIP backbone.

## Intent
**DRIP core US** — blue-chip mundial. Forever-hold.

## Business snapshot

Global CPG — 5 segmentos:
- **Beauty** (Pantene, Olay, Head & Shoulders, SK-II)
- **Grooming** (Gillette — troubled, Venus, Braun)
- **Health Care** (Oral-B, Crest, Vicks, Metamucil)
- **Fabric & Home Care** (Tide, Downy, Ariel, Febreze, Mr Clean) — **45% revenue + flagship**
- **Baby, Feminine & Family Care** (Pampers, Always, Charmin, Bounty, Tampax)

Revenue $84B, ~180 países.

## Por que detemos

1. **Streak 68y** — longest S&P 500.
2. **22 brands com $1B+ revenue** each — unmatched portfolio breadth.
3. **Working capital best-in-class** — negative cash conversion cycle.
4. **Pricing power consistent** — manages inflation passthrough year over year.
5. **R&D edge** — Crest teeth whitening, Pantene Pro-V, Tide pods innovation cycles.
6. **EM growth engine** (Asia, Africa, Latam per-capita rising).

## Moat

- **Brand premium** Tide +30% vs store brand (inelastic purchase).
- **Shelf dominance** — slotting fees + merchandising + category management.
- **Scale eficiência** — commodity procurement size.
- **R&D cadence** — innovation every 3-5 years per category.
- **Weak moat**: Gillette vs Dollar Shave Club mostrou DTC disruption possible; digital / private label secular pressure growing.

## Current state (2026-04)

- Organic growth 4-6%/y consistent (combo price + volume mix).
- Operating margin expanding (18-19% → 20%+ target).
- FX headwind EUR/BRL somewhat manageable.
- Gillette turnaround multi-year (competitive moat erosion mas stable agora).
- Health/wellness trends mixed — Oral-B expansion, Metamucil surprisingly growing.

## Invalidation triggers

- [ ] Dividend streak break (nuclear scenario)
- [ ] Organic growth < 2% 3 years (secular brand erosion)
- [ ] Volume decline > 3% 2 years consecutive sem pricing offset
- [ ] ROIC < 15% structural
- [ ] Major accounting restatement
- [ ] Activist investor break-up pressure executado

## Sizing

- Posição actual: 10 shares
- Target 5-7% sleeve US
- **Buy-and-die**
- Reinvest dividends DRIP auto
