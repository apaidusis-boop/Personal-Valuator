# 🎯 Thesis: [[JNJ]] — Johnson & Johnson

> **Dividend King** 62y+ streak. Pharma + MedTech post-Kenvue spin. Core DRIP US. Position buy-and-die candidato.

## Intent
**DRIP core US** — steady compounder, reinvest dividends via DRIP. Hold to step-up at death (ver [[wiki/tax/Tax_lot_selection]]).

## Business snapshot

Post-spin 2023 (Kenvue consumer spun-off):
- **Innovative Medicine** (pharma) — 56% revenue — oncology, immunology (Stelara!), neuroscience, infectious disease
- **MedTech** (devices) — 44% revenue — surgery, orthopedics, vision, interventional

Top products:
- Stelara (psoriasis, IBD) — $10B+ revenue, **LOE 2025** (biosimilar competition)
- Darzalex (multiple myeloma) — growing
- Tremfya
- Invega Trinza (long-acting schizophrenia)
- DePuy Synthes orthopedics

## Por que detemos

1. **62y+ dividend streak** ([[Aristocrats_Kings]] King tier).
2. **Recession-proof** demand (medical necessity).
3. **ROIC 20%+** consistente.
4. **AAA balance sheet** (only 2 US corps AAA: JNJ + MSFT).
5. **Pipeline diversificado** — multiple franchises, não single-product risk.
6. **R&D $15B+/y** (top-5 pharma globally).

## Moat

- **Brand + trust** (MedTech purchasing decisions são muitas years/decades).
- **R&D scale** — discovery + development integrado.
- **Regulatory relationships** — decades of FDA + EMA.
- **Weak moat sobre patent cliffs** — Stelara LOE 2025 é headwind real.

## Current state (2026-04)

- Stelara biosimilar competition ramping → revenue headwind $1-3B 2025-26
- Pipeline compensating: Tremfya, Carvykti, RMAT pipeline
- MedTech growing 6-8%/y (surgery recovery post-COVID + ageing demographics)
- Talc litigation largely settled ($9B 2023 plan)
- Guidance 2026 conservador mas pipeline forward solido

## Invalidation triggers

- [ ] Dividend streak break (único trigger nuclear — virtualmente impossível; seria inflexão civilizacional)
- [ ] Major pipeline failure (single product > 20% pipeline PV)
- [ ] ROIC < 12% por 2 anos (quality deterioration)
- [ ] Net debt / EBITDA > 2.5× sustained (pós-Kenvue era cash heavy)
- [ ] Fraud / accounting restatement
- [ ] CEO change + strategy pivot sem track record

## Sizing

- Posição actual: 10 shares
- Target 5-7% sleeve US equity
- **Buy-and-die holding** — não trim salvo allocation drift > 10%
- Reinvest dividendos via DRIP (JPM supports auto-DRIP)
- **Tax note**: US withhold 30% on dividend para PF BR → DY efectivo ~2.1% em vez de 3% nominal. Ver [[Dividend_withholding_BR_US]].
