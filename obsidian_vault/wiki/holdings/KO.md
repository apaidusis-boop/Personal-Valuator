# 🎯 Thesis: [[KO]] — Coca-Cola

> Dividend King 62y. Buffett canonical holding (400M+ shares via Berkshire). **Brand + route-to-market moat**. Core DRIP forever-hold.

## Intent
**DRIP core US** — steady compounder. Reinvest dividends. Buy-and-die candidate.

## Business snapshot

- Concentrate + syrup producer (asset-light IP business).
- **500+ brands globally**: Coca-Cola Original, Zero, Diet, Sprite, Fanta, Dasani, Smartwater, Powerade, Minute Maid, Costa Coffee, Topo Chico, BodyArmor, Monster minority.
- **Bottler system** — KO sells concentrate; bottlers (franchise) produce + distribute.
- Markets: 200+ países; emerging markets = growth engine.

## Por que detemos

1. **Streak 62y** + Buffett endorsement multi-décadas.
2. **Brand moat** — Coca-Cola brand avaliada $100B+ (Interbrand).
3. **Route-to-market DSD** — bottler system + cold drink equipment presence impossível replicar.
4. **Emerging markets growth** — África + Ásia per-capita consumption subida longe do plateau.
5. **Asset-light model** — concentrate sale = high margin; bottlers carregam capex.
6. **Defensive beta 0.5-0.6** — recession-resilient.

## Moat

- **Brand premium** — consumer paga 20-50% premium vs private label em >99% mercados.
- **Shelf + cooler access** (payments to retailers for placement).
- **Bottler relationships** 50+ years em muitos markets.
- **Weak moat sobre SKU Coke Classic** vs shifts health-conscious + GLP-1 secular trend.

## Current state (2026-04)

- Pricing power manifest: double-digit price/mix growth 2022-24.
- Volumes soft but positive (0-2%).
- Emerging markets (India, Southeast Asia, Africa) outperforming US/Europe.
- **GLP-1 / Ozempic** overhang: thesis says users drink less sugary drinks. KO CEO pushback: "occasion-based consumption unaffected". Real impact ainda incerto; monitorar.
- Diet/Zero portfolio growing > sugar (brand resilience).

## Invalidation triggers

- [ ] Dividend streak break (nuclear)
- [ ] GLP-1 structural demand destruction evidence (volumes -5% 2 years)
- [ ] Organic growth < 2% 3 years consecutivos
- [ ] ROIC < 15% sustained
- [ ] Loss of major market (China/India/etc regulatory)
- [ ] Governance/accounting event (historically spotless)

## Sizing

- Posição actual: 10.97 shares (with fractional from DRIP)
- Target 4-6% sleeve US
- **Buy-and-die** — never trim except severe allocation drift
- Reinvest dividends (DRIP auto via JPM)
- Tax: 30% US withhold para residente BR → net ~2% DY
