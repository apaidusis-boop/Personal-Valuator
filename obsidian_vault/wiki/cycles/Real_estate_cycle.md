# 🏢 Real Estate Cycle — framework

## Fases canónicas (Fred Harrison / Glenn Mueller)

```
Recovery → Expansion → Hyper-supply → Recession → Recovery
   │          │             │             │
Occupancy↑  Rents↑       Vacancy↑        Prices↓
Starts↑     Starts↑      Starts↓         Starts↓
Cap rate↓   Cap rate↓    Cap rate↑       Cap rate↑
```

## Fase 1: Recovery

**Sinais**:
- Occupancy bottomed, stable
- Rent growth absent or negative
- New construction starts at low
- Cap rates peaked
- Transaction volume low

**Duration**: 18-36 meses.
**Winners**: First-movers, balance sheet strong (buy distressed).
**Action**: **ACCUMULATE** quality FIIs / REITs em discount.

**Historical**:
- 2010-2012 (post-GFC US REIT recovery)
- 2021 (post-COVID office reset first signs)

## Fase 2: Expansion

**Sinais**:
- Occupancy rising quickly (+2-4pp/y)
- Rent growth +5-10%/y sustained
- New starts increasing
- Cap rates compressing
- Transaction volume rising
- Construction completion still below demand

**Duration**: 36-60 meses.
**Winners**: Operators + developers.
**Action**: **HOLD**, enjoy organic growth.

**Historical**:
- 2014-2019 (US REIT bull)
- 2015-2020 (BR FII expansion pre-COVID)

## Fase 3: Hyper-supply

**Sinais**:
- Occupancy stalling, starting to decline
- Rent growth decelerating
- New supply completing (18-24m post-start)
- Cap rates bottoming
- Transaction volume peaking

**Duration**: 12-24 meses.
**Winners**: Sellers at peak.
**Action**: **TRIM** winners, raise cash.

**Historical**:
- 2006-2007 (US pre-GFC)
- 2019-2020 (US office + retail pre-COVID)

## Fase 4: Recession

**Sinais**:
- Occupancy declining (-2-5pp/y)
- Rent concessions, TI (tenant improvements) generous
- Vacancy rising toward 10-15%
- Cap rates expanding
- Transactions stall

**Duration**: 18-36 meses.
**Losers**: Over-levered REITs, marginal locations.
**Action**: **DEFENSIVE** — blue-chip only, cash preservation.

**Historical**:
- 2007-2010 (GFC)
- 2020-2022 (COVID office shock, gradual)

## Sector-specific cycles (desincronizados)

### Office
- **2020-2026 in structural recession** (WFH permanent shift).
- US office vacancy 17-22% (San Francisco > 30%).
- Cap rates widening 2021 4-5% → 2024 7-9%.
- [[BXP]], [[VNO]], [[SLG]] peer suffering.
- BR [[KNRI11]]/[[PVBI11]] soft mas menos dramatic.

### Industrial / logística
- **Secular bull 2015-present** (e-commerce).
- Vacancy < 5% consistent.
- Rent growth 7-15%/y 2021-22, moderating now.
- US: [[PLD]], STAG, REXR. BR: [[HGLG11]], [[BRCO11]], [[XPLG11]], [[GARE11]].

### Retail / shopping
- **Secular decline** mass retail (malls), mixed strip malls.
- COVID crushed, partial recovery 2022-24.
- Grocery-anchored vs lifestyle differ greatly.
- BR: [[VISC11]], XPML11, MALL11.
- US: SPG (prime malls), FRT (strip aristocrat).

### Residencial
- US single-family demographic shortage (10M+ undersupply).
- Multifamily lagged post-COVID rent normalisation.
- AVB, EQR, MAA solid.
- BR residential institutional nascent.

### Data centers
- **Secular bull** (AI/cloud infrastructure).
- EQIX, DLR beneficiaries.
- Cycle muted by demand.

### Cell towers
- **Secular but mature** (5G/6G buildout).
- AMT, CCI, SBAC.

### Self-storage
- **Secular + defensive** (life events drive).
- PSA, EXR, CUBE.

## BR specifics

### FIIs tijolo (real estate físico)
- Selic cycle-driver: Selic ↑ = FII price ↓ (bond proxy).
- IPCA-linked rents protect real income.
- Duration ~5-7 anos equivalente.
- **Prime FII cycle indicator**: IFIX index level vs 200d MA.

### FIIs papel (CRI/CRA)
- Anti-cyclical vs Selic: Selic ↑ = DY ↑.
- Sensitive to credit quality dos originadores.
- Less real estate, more fixed income.

## US specifics

REITs sensitive to **[[10Y_Treasury]] yield**:
- 10Y 1.5% (2021) → cap rates 4% → REITs bull.
- 10Y 4.5% (2024) → cap rates 7% → REITs bear.
- Spread 10Y-to-cap rate typically 150-250 bps.

## Red flags — cycle turning bearish

- Occupancy declining 3+ consecutive quarters
- New starts accelerating amid slowing demand
- Cap rates compressing while rates rising (unsustainable)
- Transaction volume peak + spread compression

## Red flags — cycle turning bullish

- Occupancy bottom + 2 quarters stable
- New starts bottom, lowest in 5y+
- Cap rates peaking + forward rate cuts expected
- Distressed asset sales emerging

## Playbook application

- **Phase 3**: **HOLD**, trim bloated positions.
- **Phase 4**: **DEFENSIVE** — only highest quality (prime locations, fortress balance sheets).
