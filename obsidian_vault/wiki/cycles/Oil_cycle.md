# 🛢 Oil Cycle — framework

## Fases canónicas

```
           Discovery/Boom    Oversupply    Discipline    Shortage
              │                 │              │            │
Brent →  ────/\────         ────\___       ____|___      __/\__
                \                  \____            ____/
                                        \_____ ___/
                                              V
```

## Fase 1: Discovery / Boom

**Sinais**:
- Brent ≥ $80-90 sustained
- Rig count climbing rapidly (US Baker Hughes weekly)
- CAPEX guidance aumentando em majors + E&P
- M&A rumors (consolidação pre-peak)

**Duration**: 18-36 meses de avanço.
**Winners**: E&P puro (OXY, COP, EOG) outperform majors.
**Action**: TRIM positions acumuladas em trough, start rebalance OUT of energy.

**Historical**:
- 2003-2008 (China supercycle)
- 2010-2014 (shale gold rush)
- 2021-2022 (post-COVID recovery + Russia war premium)

## Fase 2: Oversupply / correção

**Sinais**:
- Supply response lagged (18m post-capex decision)
- Inventory build-ups (OECD commercial stocks)
- Brent turn from $80 → $60 → $40 rápido (3-6m)
- Capex cuts announced emergência
- Layoffs oil services

**Duration**: 6-18 meses.
**Losers**: High-cost producers (oil sands Canadian, shale marginal).
**Winners**: Integrated majors survive via refino/downstream margens.
**Action**: avoid E&P, keep [[XOM]]/[[CVX]] aristocrat dividends protected.

**Historical**:
- 2014-2016 (Saudi pump-to-kill-shale)
- 2020 (COVID demand collapse + Saudi-Russia price war)

## Fase 3: Discipline / consolidação

**Sinais**:
- Majors CEOs emphasize "capital discipline"
- CAPEX ≤ 50% FCF
- Dividend + buyback ≥ 70% FCF
- M&A para consolidar (Exxon+Pioneer 2023, Chevron+Hess 2023)
- OPEC+ production cuts coordinated
- Rig count flat/down despite higher Brent

**Duration**: 2-4 anos.
**Winners**: Majors com strong balance sheets (XOM, CVX).
**Action**: **Best entry window** para income-focused DRIP position. Aristocrat + underloved.

**Historical**:
- 2017-2019 (post-shale shakeout, Brent $50-70 discipline)
- 2023-2025 (post-Russia war discipline, Brent $75-85 discipline)

## Fase 4: Shortage / setup

**Sinais**:
- Underinvest years 2015-2020 bearing fruit
- Demand recovers + grows (EM consumption)
- OPEC+ spare capacity shrinking (< 2 Mbd)
- Inventories below 5y avg
- Long-dated futures curve steepening (contango → backwardation)
- Geopolitics prémio (Russia/Iran/Venezuelan production constraints)

**Duration**: 1-3 anos.
**Winners**: Everyone com production, E&P lever > majors.
**Action**: **Do not add em late stage**. TRIM winners near peak.

**Leading indicators**: 
- Floating storage inventory (satellite-tracked), 
- Term structure (contango collapse).

## Red flags — cycle turning bearish

- Brent > 2× 5y avg breakeven US shale → demand destruction incoming

## Red flags — cycle turning bullish

- Majors CEOs speak to "structural undersupply 2025-2028"

- Strategic Reserve depletion US < 300m barrels
