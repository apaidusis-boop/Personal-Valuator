# COVID-19 Crash 2020 — Fastest bear market ever

## Timeline

### Pre-crash (Jan-Feb 2020)
- S&P 500 ATH **3386** Feb 19 2020
- VIX em baixa 13-15
- Market complacent pese aware da crise

### Crash (Feb 20 - Mar 23 2020)
- **33 trading days**: S&P 3386 → **2237** = **−34%**
- **Fastest bear market** da história
- VIX spike para **82** (Mar 16)

### Intervenção emergency (Mar 15-23)
- **Mar 15**: Fed cuts 100bp emergency (Sunday), QE **$500B Treasuries + $200B MBS**
- **Mar 17**: CP (Commercial Paper) facility, PDCF
- **Mar 23**: **"Whatever it takes"** — unlimited QE + MLF + SMCCF (corporate bonds!)
- Fed balance sheet: $4.2T → **$7.2T em 4 meses**

### Recovery V-shape (Mar 24, 2020 onwards)
- S&P 2237 → **4600** em 18 meses = +106%
- **All-time highs** by Aug 2020
- Nasdaq +80% em 2020 alone

## O momento −$37 WTI

**Apr 20 2020** — futuros WTI Maio expiraram em **−$37/barril** (!!!) — produtores pagavam para retirar o óleo. Storage Cushing cheio.

Causas:
- Demand collapse 20% globally em semanas
- Saudi-Russia price war (flooding market)
- Physical storage completamente cheio

**Lesson**: commodity contratos físicos podem ir negativos. ETFs USO perderam muito por roll.

## Lições permanentes

### 1. Fed can prevent crashes quando wants
**Mar 2020** mostrou que Fed tem tools unlimited. "Don't fight the Fed" literal.

### 2. Velocidade > magnitude
Crash foi violent mas curto. Investors **disciplined DCA** em Mar-Apr ganharam enorme.

### 3. VIX > 50 = screaming BUY signal
Anyone who bought Mar 23 2020 doubled money em 18m.

### 4. Tech winners durable vs bubble?
Some (MSFT, GOOGL, META) durable → still up 2026
Others (ZM, DOCU, PTON) **crashed back** 2022 when WFH narrative died

### 5. Commodity extremes reversing fast
WTI $-37 → $130 em 2 anos. Cycle velocity accelerated.

### 6. Fiscal matters
US sent $1400 stimulus cheques; BR auxílio emergencial. **Direct cash** to consumers = immediate recovery. Monetary tools less effective em pandemic shock.

### 7. Debt/deficit explosion consequência
US debt/GDP: 100% → 125% em 2 anos. Seeds for 2022-2024 inflation.

## Portfolio implications

### Winners que mantiveram
- Buffett-quality Aristocrats (JNJ, PG, KO, PEP) — mild drawdown, steady recovery
- Cash-rich tech (MSFT, GOOGL, AAPL)
- Gold (+25% 2020)

### What worked (DCA)
- **Consistent monthly DCA** em S&P500 index during crash = +50% em 12m
- **Triggered buying** em VIX > 40 = historically correct action

### What failed
- Panic selling at bottom (many retail sold Mar 20-23)
- Excessive cash positions held 2020-2021 missed recovery
- Bond-heavy portfolios (60/40) underperformed

## Hoje (2026) — legacy still visible

- **Secular inflation leg** (2021-2024) consequence of stimulus
- **Housing affordability crisis** — 30y mortgages 7% while prices stayed high
- **Return-to-office trend** destroyed office REITs
- **E-commerce normalization** — pandemic boost durable but not 100%
- **Vaccine IP fights** (MRNA patent battles)
