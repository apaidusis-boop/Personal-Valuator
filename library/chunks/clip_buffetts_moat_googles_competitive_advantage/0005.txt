Google's biggest threat likely comes from significant changes that may take place in how Internet users behave. For example, the company's effectiveness would be challenged if social networks became so popular that they curtailed the usefulness of Internet searches. Effective as its moat currently is, Google must remain nimble and prepared to change with its environment to maintain and widen its advantage.
