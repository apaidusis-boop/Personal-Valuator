## THE WALT DISNEY COMPANY (NYSE:DIS)

For much of the past five years, Disney's story has been a survival story.

Linear TV declining. Streaming losses mounting. Iger back at the helm trying to right the ship before the creditors started circling. It made for gripping headlines — but it wasn't exactly an investor's dream.

That story is over.

What's emerging in its place is something genuinely exciting: a media empire that has stopped fighting the AI wave and started riding it.

Disney's Office of Technology Enablement — its internal "AI brain" — reached full operational scale in late 2025, coordinating more than 100 experts across Studios, Parks, and Streaming. In 2025, Disney's Accelerator program selected Animaj, an AI startup that can reduce animated episode production from five months to under five weeks. Disney also unveiled its Disney Select AI Engine for precision advertising targeting and Magic Words Live, a real-time contextual ad-matching tool.
