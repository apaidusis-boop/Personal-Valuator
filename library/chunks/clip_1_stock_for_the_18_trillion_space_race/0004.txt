Neutron, once operational, will face Falcon 9 head-on. Technological change in the sector moves rapidly and could render current offerings less competitive.

### 5\. Concentration and Dependency Risks

A meaningful portion of Rocket Lab's revenue is tied to government contracts, particularly with the U.S. Department of Defense and agencies like NASA. Changes in federal budget priorities, contract cancellations, or shifts in defense spending could materially impact revenue. Additionally, the company still depends on a relatively limited number of large customers.
