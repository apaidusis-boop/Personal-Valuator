Existing growth rate and excess returns.
 Momentum does matter, when it comes to projecting
growth. Firms that have been reporting rapidly growing revenues are more likely to see revenues
grow rapidly at least in the near future. Firms that are earning high returns on capital and high
excess returns in the current period are likely to sustain these excess returns for the next few
years.
