The Tax Benefits of R&D Expensing
The preceding chapter argued that R&D expenses should be capitalized. If we decide to do so,
however, there is a tax benefit that we might be missing. Firms are allowed to deduct their entire
R&D expense for tax purposes. In contrast, they are allowed to deduct only the depreciation on their
