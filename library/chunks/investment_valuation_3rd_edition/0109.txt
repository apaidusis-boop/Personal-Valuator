The Model
While the derivation of the Black-Scholes model is far too complicated to present here, it is based on
the idea of creating a portfolio of the underlying asset and the riskless asset with the same cash flows,
and hence the same cost, as the option being valued. The value of a call option in the Black-Scholes
model can be written as a function of the five variables:
