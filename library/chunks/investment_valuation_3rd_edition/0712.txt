This total beta estimate, in a sense, takes the limiting view that the potential buyer will own only Chez Pierre. To the extent
that the buyer has some diversification, the correlation coefficient will be adjusted upward; if the buyer has a diversified
portfolio, the correlation coefficient will approach 1 and the total beta will converge on the market beta.
