But what about firms that have betas well below 1, such as commodity companies? If you are
assuming that these firms will stay in their existing businesses, there is no harm in assuming that the
beta remains at existing levels. However, if your estimates of growth in perpetuity will require them
to branch out into other businesses, you should adjust the beta upward toward 1; invoking another rule
of thumb, stable period betas should not be lower than 8.
