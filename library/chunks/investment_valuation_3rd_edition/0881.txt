Increasing Risk Can Increase Equity Value
In traditional discounted cash flow valuation, higher risk almost always translates into lower value
for equity investors. When equity takes on the characteristics of a call option, you should not expect
this relationship to continue to hold. Risk can become your ally, when you are an equity investor in a
troubled firm. In essence, you have little to lose and much to gain from swings in firm value.
