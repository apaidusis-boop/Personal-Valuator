Application
Having established the fundamentals that determine the enterprise value to EBITDA multiple, we can
now examine how best to apply the multiple. The multiple is most widely used in capital-intensive
firms with heavy infrastructure investments. The rationale that is given for using the multiple—that
EBITDA is the operating cash flow of the firm—does not really hold up, because many of these firms
also tend to have capital expenditure needs that drain cash flows. There are, however, good reasons
for using this multiple when depreciation methods vary widely across firms and the bulk of the
investment in infrastructure has already been made.
