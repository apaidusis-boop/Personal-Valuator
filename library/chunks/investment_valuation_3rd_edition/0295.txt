Estimating Expected Changes in Noncash Working Capital
 While we can estimate the noncash
working capital change fairly simply for any year using financial statements, this estimate has to be
used with caution. Changes in noncash working capital are unstable, with big increases in some years
followed by big decreases in subsequent years. To ensure that the projections are not the result of an
