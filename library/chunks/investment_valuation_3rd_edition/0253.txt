was also $10 billion. The marginal tax rate was 40%.
a.
 Estimate the unlevered beta for Time Warner.

beta of the stock.

a.
 Estimate the unlevered beta of the firm.
b.
 Estimate the effect of paying out a special dividend of $5 billion on this unlevered beta.
c.
 Estimate the beta for Chrysler after the special dividend.
