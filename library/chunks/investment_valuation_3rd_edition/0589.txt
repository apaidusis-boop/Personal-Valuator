Just as the price-to-sales ratio is determined by net profit margins, payout ratios, and costs of
equity, the value-to-sales ratio is determined by after-tax operating margins, reinvestment rates, and
the cost of capital. Firms with higher operating margins (ATOM), lower reinvestment rates (for any
given growth rate), and lower costs of capital will trade at higher value-to-sales multiples.
