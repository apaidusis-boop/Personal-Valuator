Approach 2: Using the Binomial Model

The binomial model is much more capable of handling early exercise because it considers the cash
flows at each time period, rather than just at expiration. The biggest limitation of the binomial model
is determining what stock prices will be at the end of each period, but this can be overcome by using
a variant that allows us to estimate the up and the down movements in stock prices from the estimated
variance. There are four steps involved:
