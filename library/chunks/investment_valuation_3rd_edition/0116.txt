Approach 1: Pseudo-American Valuation
Step 1:
 Define when dividends will be paid and how much the dividends will be.
Step 2:
 Value the call option to each ex-dividend date using the dividend-adjusted approach
described earlier, where the stock price is reduced by the present value of expected dividends.
Step 3:
 Choose the maximum of the call values estimated for each ex-dividend day.
