## What Is a Robo-Advisor?

A [robo-advisor](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/r/roboadvisor-roboadviser.asp) is a digital platform that enables [automated investing](https://www.investopedia.com/what-is-automated-investing-7569400), offering financial planning and wealth management services with minimal human involvement. Their services often include the following:

- Client onboarding
- Investment strategy
- [Asset allocation](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/a/assetallocation.asp)
- Regular rebalancing
- [Tax-loss harvesting](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/t/taxgainlossharvesting.asp)
