### How to Use These Metrics

**To understand risk and reward:** What kind of ride should you buckle up for if you decide to add shares to your portfolio? Our quant return and risk estimates are designed to give you a snapshot of a stock's likely performance characteristics for the year ahead.

**To help decide if a stock is right for you:** What's more important than buying the right stock? Sometimes, it's *not buying* the wrong stock! Because our estimates are designed to provide you with our quant team's "best guess" at a stock's likely return stats, they can help you identify stocks that don't fit well with your investor type.

When you're evaluating a stock for your portfolio, if you're not comfortable with seeing annual returns that fall within our expected return range or potentially enduring a one-year drawdown on the scale of our estimated maximum drawdown, the stock might not be right for you.
