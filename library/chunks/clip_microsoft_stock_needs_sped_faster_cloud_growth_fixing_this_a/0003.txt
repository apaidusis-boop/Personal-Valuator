Aerospace investors can be glad they weren’t in software stocks. The sector continues to be hammered by fears that AI tools will take market share of the enterprise software market. [ServiceNow](https://www.barrons.com/market-data/stocks/now?mod=article_chiclet) was the poster child for the problem. Shares dropped almost 18% after its quarterly [earnings report](https://www.barrons.com/articles/servicenow-earnings-stock-price-4b5f8eee?mod=article_inline). That might be considered unfairly punished since full-year 2026 earnings estimates are down only four cents, or about 1%, since earnings.
