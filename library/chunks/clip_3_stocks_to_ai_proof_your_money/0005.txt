## 2\. Comfort Systems (FIX): The Pick-and-Shovel Play AI Can't Disrupt

When ChatGPT launched in November 2022, most investors looked to chipmakers, cloud platforms, and software companies for the windfall. Few looked at an HVAC contractor headquartered in Houston, Texas.

Comfort Systems USA traded at roughly $115 per share when ChatGPT went live. As of March 2026, it trades above $1,400.
