## ROKU (NASDAQ:ROKU)

Roku has always been the Switzerland of streaming wars.

While Netflix, Disney, and Amazon fought over content supremacy, Roku quietly became the infrastructure those wars run on. Today, with 90 million streaming households and a 37% share of the connected TV device market in North America, Roku isn't just a platform — it's the platform. More U.S. viewing time happens on Roku-powered devices than on traditional broadcast television.

And that position becomes more valuable, not less, as AI reshapes the advertising industry.
